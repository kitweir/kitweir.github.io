BIO

I’m a UX designer, planted and grown in Vancouver, BC. My identity is a keystone of my practice: I’m a chronically ill, mixed-race designer from a low-income family, often navigating spaces where these are not the norm: I’m an edge case in most contexts. This has developed my passion for socially responsible design and my unique approach with my work, which is characterized by sensitivity, care, and consideration of often overlooked perspectives.

My education in both UX Design and Business Administration allows me to bridge the gap between users and businesses; with empathy for both contexts, I tackle projects with equal parts strategic mind and creative heart.

Interested in working with me? Check out my resume, or say hello at hello@kitweir.com.
